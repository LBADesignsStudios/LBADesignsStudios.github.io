From Concept to Finished Product

I don't just design. I manufacture in-house. Let's take a look at what it took to get me to this point.

The beginning: Vinyl Prints

Started with my wife's vinyl cutter, printing single color designs. That foundation gave me the vision for better quality and longer lasting products.

Social Media Manager

I took the skills I learned from managing my own social media accounts and took over as Social Media Manager for Motor City Brewing Works in 2018. Over four years I grew social media following and engagement over 40%.

Large Orders, No Problem

Fulfilled screen printing orders of 100+ t-shirts for local companies, schools, and events.

Total Package

Proficient in Adobe Suite: Photoshop, Premiere, After Effects. Great with Canva, Microsoft Office Suite, Pro Tools, FL Studio and more. I create complete brand experiences.
